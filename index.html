Having a Wild Time at Work

Over the summer I went back home to work at the Kansas City Zoo. This was my second summer working there for the Admissions team. The majority of my job responsibilities revolved around selling tickets and manning the guest relations desk in the front office. My job also allowed me to work with over 50 stingrays and 9 white spotted bamboo sharks in our Stingray Bay Building. Over the past year, we've had nearly 20 stingray babies born at our facility.
